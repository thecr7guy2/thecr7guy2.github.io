I trained GPT-2 small from scratch to understand the full loop: data, tokenizer, training stability, and evaluation.

## What mattered most

- Data quality and deduping mattered more than architectural tweaks.
- A stable input pipeline prevented GPU starvation and noisy loss.
- Frequent lightweight eval checkpoints helped avoid wasted long runs.

## Why this project matters

This project forced me to connect model quality with real engineering constraints: I/O, memory, and experiment discipline.
